In blackjack, your goal is to get a hand value close to 21 without going over. Each player and the dealer are dealt two cards. Number cards are worth their face value, face cards are 10, and Aces are 1 or 11. You choose to "hit" (take a card) or "stand" (keep your hand). If you go over 21, you lose. After players finish, the dealer reveals their hidden card and must hit until reaching at least 17. The closest hand to 21 without busting wins. If you have an Ace and a 10-value card as your first two cards, it's called a "blackjack" and usually pays more. The game is about beating the dealer without busting.
